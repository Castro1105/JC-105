Wonder

I will be reviewing the book Wonder. I have read this book many times and is honestly one of the best books I have read. More specifically, I will be describing some main characters such as Auggie and a friend named Jack

Auggie

Auggie is the main character of this book. He is a young kid that has facial differences and has been home schooled until his parents finally decided it was time for him to go to his local school. He meets new friends as well as some bullies along the way but he overcomes it.

Jack

Jack is one of Auggie's best friends that he met throughout the story. What separated Jack from the other classmates is how he quickly accepted Auggie despite his difference as well as going above and beyond as defending Auggie from his bully. "Do you ever want to beat those kids up?"-Jack
