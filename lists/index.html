Definitions

Alternator
A part from a vehicle that supplies electricity to be stored in a vehicles battery.
Differential
A system that allows the drive wheels to turn into a different position.
Transmission
Acts as a medium that transmits power generated by the engine to the wheels via a mechanical system of of gears and gear trains.
Axle
A rod or shaft that connects a pair of wheels to propel them and retain the position of the wheels to one another.
Radiator
The key component of the engines cooling system.
